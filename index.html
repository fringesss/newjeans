On July 1, 2019, HYBE (then-known as Big Hit) announced that Min Hee Jin would join the company as their new CBO (Chief Brand Officer), and one of her tasks is to lead a girl group project between Big Hit and Source Music. On September 4, 2019, the Plus Global Auditions were announced. Auditions were held in 16 cities and lasted until the end of October. By the end of 2019, casting had been completed and training began at the beginning of 2020. By September 2021, the girl group's management was transferred to ADOR. On November 21, 2021, HYBE officially announced the launch of a new independent label, ADOR, under the agency.
